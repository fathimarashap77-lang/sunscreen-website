Sunscreen

Sunscreen is one of the most important steps in skincare because it protects our skin from the harmful effects of the sun’s ultraviolet (UV) rays. Daily use of sunscreen helps prevent sunburn, premature aging like wrinkles and dark spots, and even serious problems such as skin cancer. It works by either absorbing or reflecting UV rays before they can damage the skin. Dermatologists recommend using a broad-spectrum sunscreen with at least SPF 30 and reapplying it every two hours when outdoors. Choosing the right sunscreen for your skin type—gel-based for oily skin, hydrating creams for dry skin, and gentle, fragrance-free formulas for sensitive skin—ensures comfort and effectiveness. Applying sunscreen regularly, even on cloudy days, keeps the skin healthy, youthful, and well-protected.
